Case Study: A Patient Safety App - A Digital Transformation Journey.

In the fast-paced world of veterinary care, ensuring patient safety isn’t just a priority—it’s a mission. Leading a $1.2M digital transformation, I spearheaded the continued development and deployment of a custom ServiceNow application that redefined how 15,000 hospitals across 10+ countries track, analyze, and prevent patient safety incidents. Through Agile SaaS innovation, automated workflows, and real-time analytics, we empowered veterinary teams with data-driven insights, driving proactive decision-making and safeguarding the well-being of countless animals. This project wasn’t just about technology—it was about making a lasting impact on patient care and operational excellence.
