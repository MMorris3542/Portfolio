Case Study: Illusions of Gold, to Ground Zero - Building an Enterprise Project Management Office

What began as a dream assignment—stepping into a “gold standard” PMO—quickly unraveled into a high-stakes wake-up call. Behind the polished facade lay outdated processes, audit flags, and systemic risk. Seizing the opportunity, I led the charge to dismantle the illusion and rebuild from the ground up. This case study showcases how I established a compliant, scalable, and outcomes-driven PMO framework—driving strategic alignment, restoring trust, and delivering over 50 projects annually across business, technology, and compliance domains. It’s not just about building structure—it’s about building belief, capability, and a future-proof foundation.
